Chemistry Lab

Our school’s Chemistry Laboratory is a well-equipped space where students explore the fascinating world of chemicals and reactions. It is designed with modern safety measures, high-quality apparatus, and sufficient working space to conduct a wide range of experiments. Under the guidance of experienced teachers, students develop practical skills, scientific thinking, and a deeper understanding of concepts taught in the classroom.

Physics Lab

Our school’s Physics Laboratory is a modern and well-organized space where students explore the principles of physics through hands-on experiments. Equipped with up-to-date instruments and safety measures, it helps learners understand concepts such as mechanics, electricity, optics, and magnetism in a practical way. Guided by skilled teachers, students develop scientific thinking, problem-solving abilities, and an enthusiasm for innovation.

Biology Lab

Our school’s Biology Laboratory is a spacious and well-equipped learning environment where students explore the wonders of life sciences. It is stocked with modern microscopes, models, preserved specimens, and charts that help students study plants, animals, and human anatomy. Guided by experienced teachers, learners gain practical skills, observe experiments firsthand, and develop a scientific understanding of living organisms and their processes.
